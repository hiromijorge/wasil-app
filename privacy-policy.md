# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 23, 2026

**Wasil** ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Wasil mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information we collect

### Account information
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo (optional)

### Location information
- Delivery/pickup addresses you save or enter
- Device location when you choose to use "Use my current location"

### Payment information
- Bank transfer receipt images you upload
- We do **not** collect credit/debit card information or bank account credentials

### Device information
- Push notification token
- Device type and operating system

### Order and usage data
- Products you browse, cart, and purchase
- Messages you exchange with merchants
- Order history and delivery status

## 2. How we use your information

- To provide marketplace services (browse, order, deliver)
- To connect customers with merchants and drivers
- To verify payments and process orders
- To send order updates and messages via push notifications
- To improve the App and customer support

## 3. Sharing your information

We share information only with:
- **Merchants** — to fulfill your orders (name, phone, address)
- **Drivers** — to complete deliveries (name, phone, pickup/drop-off address)
- **Service providers** — Supabase (hosting, database, storage, authentication)

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

## 5. Security

We use industry-standard measures including Supabase authentication, Row Level Security (RLS), and encrypted connections. However, no method is 100% secure.

## 6. Your choices

- Update profile information in the App settings
- Disable push notifications in device settings
- Contact us to delete your account

## 7. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
